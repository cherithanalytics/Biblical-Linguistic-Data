4
1 For, behold, a day comes burning as an oven, and it shall consume them; and all the aliens, and all that do wickedly, shall be stubble: and the day that is coming shall set them on fire, says the Lord Almighty, and there shall not be left of them root or branch. 2 But to you that fear my name shall the Sun of righteousness arise, and healing shall be in his wings: and you° shall go forth, and bound as young calves let loose from bonds. 3 And you° shall trample the wicked; for they shall be ashes underneath your feet in the day which I appoint, says the Lord Almighty. 4 And, behold, I will send to you Elias the Thesbite, before the great and glorious day of the Lord comes; 5  ✡ Luk 1. 17. who shall turn again the heart of the father to the son, and the heart of a man to his neighbor, lest I come and strike the earth grievously. 6 Remember the law of my servant Moses, accordingly as I charged him with it in Choreb for all Israel, even the commandments and ordinances.
